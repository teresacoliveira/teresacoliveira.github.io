## 👩‍💼 About Me
I am a biochemist and cell biologist at the **Center for Neuroscience and Cell Biology (CNC-UC)** and the **Center for Innovative Biomedicine and Biotechnology (CiBB)** at the University of Coimbra, Portugal. 

My scientific trajectory spans over two decades, evolving from foundational benchwork in mitochondrial physiology, metabolism, and toxicology to a broader mission in **metascience, data stewardship, and Open Science**. 

By blending experimental biomedicine with data-driven workflows, I focus on raising data literacy among laboratory researchers, developing automated screening tools for research rigor, and designing workflows that optimize documentation, reproducibility, and transparency across scientific teams.

### 🛠️ Technical Skillset & Focus Areas
* **Core Science:** Cell Biology, Mitochondrial Physiology, Metabolism, Toxicology, Neuroscience.
* **Data Science & Analytics:** SQL, Python, R, Power BI, Computational Biology.
* **Metascience:** Research Data Management (RDM), FAIR & Open Science principles, Reporting Practices, Quality-Management Processes.
